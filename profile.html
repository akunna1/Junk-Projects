About Me

I am a Software Engineer (Full Stack) with Data Science Expertise, currently working as a freelancer under the business name, Akunna Tech Solutions. Originally from Nigeria, my educational and professional journey started in North Carolina, driven by my pursuit of opportunities. I launched this guidebook for my portfolio in order to educate others about web & mobile development and data science, while showcasing my projects and personal journey. My coding journey began during my undergraduate studies at UNC Chapel Hill, where I discovered my passion for geographic information systems (GIS) and urban data analytics, which laid the foundations for my interest and devotion to data science and full stack development.
