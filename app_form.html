About Our University

In the shadowy corridors of the University of Blackmourne Hollows at Shadowbrook, a haunting influence lingers, whispering of its power to shape the most obscure minds among a diverse congregation burdened by the impending weight of global consequence. Amidst relentless research, eerie pedagogy, and the mournful echoes of civic duty, undergraduate and graduate education begets knowledge steeped in darkness, unfurling twisted innovations to heal the wounds of a tormented society. Founded in 1701, in Shadowbrook, Hauntsylvania, this institution reigns over a realm of foreboding and chilling degrees.

The University of Blackmourne Hollows at Shadowbrook, shrouded in perpetual shadows, extends its reach across the abyss, offering bachelor's, master's, and doctoral degrees that delve into the very depths of despair. With over 30 departments, our students descend further into the abyss, where they toil in silence, toiling closely with faculty members whose existence is as bleak as the void itself. In this foresaken realm, research is conducted, not as a pursuit of knowledge, but as a relentless journey through the nightmarish corridors of countless campus research institutes and centers, where answers are elusive, and the darkness envelopes all. External reviews, conducted every six years, serve as harbingers of impending doom, evaluating the quality of our research and teaching, while recommending ways to further intensify the darkness that permeates our programs' malevolent effectiveness.

Personal Statements

Choose Three Questions and Provide Your Responses (Max 1200 words per each document).

1. Recall a moment when you found yourself ensnared in a moral quandary of considerable weight. How did you navigate the treacherous waters, and what scars did you bear as souvenirs of the experience?

2. Detail a juncture in your life when the shadows of failure cast a chilling pall over your path. What haunting lessons emerged from the depths of this setback, etching themselves into the fabric of your character and perception?

3. If you were granted the unsettling privilege of a conversation with a historical figure, living or departed, who would emerge from the recesses of history's crypts? What unsettling discourse would transpire, and how might the specter of this communion haunt your life's purpose and destiny?

4. In the ever-darkening tapestry of our interconnected world, how do you grapple with the weight of global citizenship? What burdens of experience or harrowing perspectives have imbued you with a harrowing sense of global responsibility?

5. Envision a dystopian future where you possess the unsettling power to confront an insurmountable enigma or confront a malevolent force. What bleak conundrum would you dare to confront, and how would you navigate the abyss in your quest for resolution?

6. Delve into the depths of a tome, artwork, or scientific theorem that has cast a chilling shadow upon your psyche. How has this intellectual haunting shaped the contours of your worldview or sown the seeds of despair that sprout into your academic pursuits?

7. If the burden of designing a course or educational program fell upon your shoulders, what macabre vision would you conjure from the void? How would this eerie curriculum ensnare the unsuspecting minds of students and usher them toward the precipice of personal metamorphosis?
